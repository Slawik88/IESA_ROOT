Additional Information

How Points are Calculated

Your activity level is determined by a combination of contributions:

  • Posts: 10 points per published post
  • Likes Received: 2 points per like on your posts
  • Comments: 1 point per comment you make

Points are recalculated daily and reflect your current activity.

Tips for Advancement
  • Quality over Quantity: Focus on meaningful posts
  • Engage Regularly: Consistent participation builds reputation
  • Support Others: Comments and feedback help the community
  • Be Authentic: Share genuine insights and experiences
